Hi there! My name is Douglas and I'm a first year mechatronics engineering student at the University of Waterloo.


Previously, I've worked as a bioinformatics software developer for Agriculture Canada, developing DNA analysis pipelines, and I currently serve as an embedded programmer on both the Waterloo Midnight Sun Solar Car and Waterloo Hybrid Formula SAE teams.


I love everything from getting my hands dirty in the machine shop, to staying up all night trying to push that last bit of code to my project repository! I'm interested in embedded software, autonomous robotics, machine learning, and computer vision.
