Résumé

Hi there! My name is Douglas and I'm an engineering student studying mechatronics from the University of Waterloo. Previously, I've worked as a bioinformatics software developer for Agriculture Canada, where I wrote scripts to analyze plant DNA, and currently, I am a member of the Waterloo Midnight Sun Solar Car Team as an embedded programmer and hardware engineer. I love everything from getting my hands dirty in the machine shop, to staying up all night trying to push that last bit of code to my project repository. I'm interested in the fields of autonomous robotics, machine learning, and computer vision.
